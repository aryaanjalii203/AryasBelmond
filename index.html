Our Story

It started with one heart,
made of forty beads.

Arya's Belmond began at a study desk — one keychain, strung between lectures, gifted to a friend. She asked for two more. Her friends asked for ten. Today every bag, mini bag and heart still passes through the same two hands, one bead at a time.

Nothing here is factory-made. Choose your colours, and we'll string a piece that exists nowhere else in the world.
